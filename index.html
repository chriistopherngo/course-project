Who are we?

We are a school that loves music! With more than 20 years of experience teaching piano, we really know our stuff.

We are based at Madla Bydelshus, midway between Amfi Madla and UiS, and we accept large and small students in addition to our department at Klepp.

Yes, that's right - we have something for everyone, whether you're a child, teenager or adult!

We teach classical piano, rhythmic piano and even playing by notation/chords. And you can trust us when we say it's never boring with us.

We also have a large library with a variety of sheet music and training material in different styles and levels.

And don't forget – we have fantastic equipment! At our school you will find a concert piano.

And as if that wasn't enough, we have two concert performances a year! There is a summer concert and a Christmas concert, and you can be sure that it is always a great experience for everyone who attends.

Our private music school has taught piano at an affordable price, quality and reputation in the community for more than 20 years.
